About Us

ISM Corporation is an international marketing organization based in Hyderabad, India. Our company is led by a team of seasoned professionals with experience in marketing, sales, and finance. We operate on the principle of collective wisdom to drive our strategic decisions and business operations.

Our People

Vivek Malhotra

Vivek has 3 decades of expertise in conceptualizing, assessing, and executing contraceptive social marketing initiatives across diverse regions such as India, Uganda, Kenya, Zambia, Sri Lanka, Tanzania, Nepal, and Sudan. Currently at the helm of ISM Corporation Pvt Ltd as its Director, he has steered the organization into a prominent role as a leading social marketing entity. ISM, under his stewardship, has established itself as a significant provider of cost-effective family planning and reproductive health commodities to countless couples nationwide

A distinguished chartered accountant accredited by The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, Vivek commenced his professional journey within the financial realm at Population Services International. His trajectory led him to assume the role of Country Director for MSI Reproductive Choices in Uganda in 1995, before returning to India in 1999 to establish and lead PHSI.

P. Srinivas

Srinivas, the current Director of Sales at ISM Corporation Pvt Ltd (ISMI), boasts over 23 years of strong experience in sales, marketing, and business management across diverse sectors. Commencing his journey as a Front-Line Sales Manager in PHSI, Srinivas has ascended the organizational hierarchy, garnering profound insights into the business landscape and strategic framework. His unwavering dedication and steadfast commitment are exemplified by his adept management of a large sales force.

In his capacity as Director of Sales, Srinivas orchestrates all sales-related endeavours, encompassing marketing initiatives, talent acquisition, and the implementation of varied sales strategies aimed at fortifying ISM's competitive stance amidst industry rivals. Instrumental to the organization's expansion trajectory, he possesses a wealth of expertise in devising sales strategies and driving business growth. Drawing from an extensive collection of sales roles, on-time delivery, and compliance.

Ashutosh Kaushik

Mr. Ashutosh Kaushik has over two decades of distinguished experience across the corporate and development sectors. He has held senior positions within various international non-profit organizations. For the past ten years, Mr. Kaushik has specialized in the field of Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR), concentrating on contraception and safe abortion services. Currently, Mr. Kaushik serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the Foundation for Reproductive Health Services India (FRHS India).

Under his leadership, FRHS India is committed to providing high-quality family planning and reproductive health services through diverse delivery channels. In addition to his role at FRHS India, Mr. Kaushik is a board member of the organization and acts as a trustee for the Sarvajan Unnati Bodhini Trust, a non-profit entity dedicated to comprehensive community development.
