What we do

How We Make a Difference

At ISM Corporation, we are dedicated to improving sexual & reproductive health with informed choice and access to affordable care for individuals and families across India and emerging global markets.

Our Objective is to make essential reproductive healthcare products affordable and accessible across developing countries by partnering with trusted manufacturers. We ensure that all products are developed cost-effectively while maintaining global quality standards.

Through social marketing and a robust retail network, we ensure that people everywhere—from remote rural villages to bustling urban centres—have the required information, products, and support to build a healthy family.

Making contraceptives accessible

At ISM Corporation, we believe access begins with affordability. By pricing products based on local purchasing power, we make high-quality contraceptives and reproductive health products accessible to all.

Through our diverse product range and nationwide distribution network, we meet the needs of different income groups, offering both premium and affordable options. From condoms, contraceptive pills, IUDs, and safe abortion kits, we empower individuals to make informed choices—without facing severe financial barriers.

We make family planning products affordable and accessible for all

Through innovative social marketing and an expansive retail network, we deliver essential sexual and reproductive health (SRH) solutions—including a full range of contraceptives and menstrual hygiene products—across both Over the counter (OTC) and ethical channels.

We are one of the world’s leading providers of contraceptives and gynaecological hormones, offering one of the most comprehensive portfolios globally. From short-acting reversible methods to long-acting solutions, we empower individuals to make informed choices about their reproductive health.
