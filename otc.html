Contraceptives

Khushi contraceptive pills are safe, easy to use, effective, and act as reversible contraceptive for women wanting to delay their first pregnancy or space the next child. It comes with extra icon.

Khushi Low Dose pills are used to prevent pregnancy. These are multiple benefits of low dose pills like - maintaining regular periods, less menstrual cramps, less severe premenstrual syndrome (PMS).

Medical Abortion Kit

Khushi MT Kit

The Khushi MT Kit includes Mifepristone Tablet IP and Misoprostol Tablet IP. It is intended for use in early medical abortion up to 9 weeks (63 days) of pregnancy. The kit functions by medically terminating the pregnancy through a prescribed process.
